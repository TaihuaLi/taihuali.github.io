Why data science?

   In college, I was intrigued by how much economics theories can explain real-world phenomenon with equations. However, I was puzzled by one question, "how did economists come up with equations", which led me to study econometrics, and that was when I determined to pursue data science as career. I chose data science not only because of its mathematical charm but also due to its ability to help solve problems in an autonomous way. Data science also helps me to discover and validate findings that might not have been previously discovered, which further helps me to acquire new industry knowledge.
