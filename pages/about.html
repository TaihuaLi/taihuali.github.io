Why Data Science

    When I was in college, I LOVED how economic theories and models explain real-world phenomena. However, I was puzzled by how economists came up with the mathematical models, which led me to study econometrics and eventually to study machine learning. What machine learning attracts me the most is its ability to discover patterns in data using intuitively simple methodologies. I am also fascinated by how data science as a whole help organizations evolve their processes, services and products, which directly or indirectly impact how we live our lives.
